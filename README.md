This is a simple research project management system. Here are the functionalities:
Core Functionalities
a. Researcher & Project Management
• Add and manage researcher profiles
• Register research projects and status (ongoing/completed/published)
• Assign collaborators and advisors
b. Publication Tracking
• Store research papers, journals, and conference presentations
• Link research projects to publications
• Filter by year, department, or citation count
c. Funding Management
• Manage research grants and funding agencies
• Track expenditures and budgets
d. Collaboration & Reports
• Track inter-departmental and international collaborations
• Store MOUs and joint agreements
• Generate research output reports by department or year
